About Me

Hello! I'm an enthusiastic problem solver with a passion for building structures and turning designs into a reality.

I take personal development seriously, and I don't hesitate to explore new software and solutions that will help me reach my goals. In my pursuit of knowledge, I've learned how to use Godot and GDScript, HTML and CSS, JavaScript, and NextJS. In addition, I study interesting concepts along the way such as the advantages and pitfalls of object-oriented programming, or how to improve the accuracy of AI image recognition.
